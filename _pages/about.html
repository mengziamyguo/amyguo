--- permalink: / title: "About Me" author_profile: true redirect_from: - /about/ - /about.html --- I'm a fifth-year PhD student in department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research at University of California, Berkeley, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Zuo-Jun Max Shen. Prior to joining Berkeley, I received my B.S. degree in Applied Mathematics and Industrial Engineering & Management Science from Northwestern University in 2020.

Research Interests

My research aims to address pricing challenges that arise from the incorporation of strategic consumer behaviors, with a particular emphasis on reference effects. In both monopolistic and competitive markets, I explore techniques from online learning, game theory, and sequential decision-making to develop pricing policies that provide theoretical guarantees.
